Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Zero Data Processing Guarantee: All financial values, expenses, incomes, and mathematical calculations processed on this website execute purely client-side via your web browser. We never track, log, save, transmit, or parse your private financial records onto an external server.

1. Information We Collect

We do not manage individual profile systems or access identification elements. However, generic platform systems run the following basic configurations:

  • Log Diagnostic Files: Standard browser diagnostic files tracking your dynamic client configurations, connection time markers, and routing files.
  • Ad Network and Analytic Tracking Modules: Third-party tracking scripts, such as Google Analytics or integrated advertising networks, may use system cookies to monitor traffic flows and deliver advertisements customized to your session parameters.

2. Data Preservation Limits

Since data entry occurs natively in your browser memory stack, closing out your individual window kills all specific parameters processed during that interface instance.

3. External Link Routing Outlets

Our platform tools contain navigation points routing to third-party tools or external resource indexes. We do not maintain oversight across external architectures and urge you to review the processing metrics of those networks independently.

4. Contact Point

If you have questions regarding these functional disclosures, contact our privacy registry via our dedicated contact system interface.
